Note that the numbers in the table above only appy to the parts, the accessories have different stats. Also note that all numbers on this page are relative as exact amounts change with level and shield type.
"Special" is the shield's special effect, which changes based on shield type. It is actually made of multiple different values, but they are almost always the same, so they are shown as one.

Shields are made out of 4 parts:
AccessoryBatteryBodyCapacitor
Internally the Accessory and Body were swapped at some point, leading to the misleading names.

Accessory

The accessory decides what type of shield it is. Its model is entirely based on manufacturer however, Spike and Nova accessories of the same manufacturer use the same model.
Unique shields each have their own accessory. These accessories generally change stats from the base version, but sometimes they'll also change the model to that of a different manufacturer.
The Hide of Terramorphous is the only shield with more than one possible accessory. It affects the Hide's extra special stats, Nova Radius and Spike Damage, which aren't affected by any other special boosts. The different accessories have bonuses of -5, -3, 0, +3, and +5.

Battery, Body, and Capacitor

The other parts of a shield only affect its stats, as can be seen in the table at the top of the page.
All parts of the same brand have the same effect. This means a shield with a Dahl battery and Pangolin body has the same stats as if it had a Pangolin battery and Dahl body.
Maliwan capacitors always provide fire, shock, or corrosive resistance. Torgue capacitors always provide explosive resistance. As neither of these capacitors have a model, this is the best way to tell them apart.
Maliwan and Pangolin batteries use the same model, and unfortunatly the only way to tell them apart is through the shield's stats.

There are a few unique shield parts:
The Evolution and Neogenator can spawn with a unique Anshin battery that provides +5 max health, rather than the normal +2. It still only gives +2 to elemental resistance however.
The Kala and Transformer always have a unique Maliwan capacitor that provides shock resistance but no increase in special.
The Sunshine always has a unique Maliwan capacitor that provides fire resistance at no cost.
Any Anshin parts that spawn on the Bigg Thumppr will provide +5 "Special02". However, being a Roid Shield, it only has one special stat, so this makes no difference over the normal Anshin parts.

Material

The material of a shield is based on its rarity.
Unique shields have their own materials. Like accessories these will generally change stats, and sometimes they'll be based on a different material than the shield's rarity.
